  • Start Diving BEGINNER COURSES

    Your adventure begins here. Marvel at the magic of a kelp forest. Watch an octopus change color before your eyes. Explore the remains of an ancient shipwreck. Float weightlessly in a teeming world of colorful coral and fish. Smile as friendly sea lions pass. A manta ray soars by. Become a scuba diver. Dive In Today!

  • Let's Go Diving Destinations

    Slowly descend into warm, clear turquoise ocean guided by attentive, skilled Pros. Marvel at giant corals while floating effortlessly on a drift dive. Feel the rush on a shark dive. Watch bioluminescence on a full-moon night. New friends, memories for life. From Malibu to the Maldives, 1000’s of choices, come dive with us!

  • Get The Kit GEAR SELECTION

    Regulators that breath effortlessly at depth. Fins to glide like a Navy Seal. A wireless computer tracks & reports back. A dive suit, warm and comfortable. Wide, strong beams of rechargeable lights. HD, 3D, GoPro, DSLR. Rinse, dry, stow, ready to go. Latest, coolest & safest gear - we help you find what’s best for you.

  • Beyond Basics KEEP LEARNING

    Post an awesome dive photo. Go deep safely. Stay longer on Nitrox or a Rebreather. Be a better Dive Buddy. Navigate easily & spot-on. Train & be ready for the unexpected. Find sunken treasure. Challenge yourself. Is it a Green or White Abalone? Step up your skills. More adventure, more fun. Continue your journey now!

  • Go Pro TEACH DIVING

    Be a role model, trusted mentor. Help a diver conquer a challenge. Laugh each day, get paid to dive. Learn something new. Travel to exotic locales. Participate, shape & protect the underwater world. Serve our nation. Conduct research, make a difference. Love what you do. Look forward every day to the ocean, your office.

Welcome to World Famous Malibu Divers

Since 1969, as a founding member of the diving community that helped establish both SCUBA diving & Freediving in Los Angeles, California. Malibu Divers offers PADI SCUBA diving & Freediving training, equipment, travel and services. Our passion for the highest quality instruction, from beginner to professional divers is reflected in our expert leadership, excellent PADI training, leading-edge equipment consultation, hand-crafted career programs, and deep in-water savvy. Malibu Divers is the only Dive Center in the western USA to have earned PADI's highest rating; an IDC, Instructor Development Center. We train the World’s Best Divers and Dive Professionals.
