Coastal Cleanup Day

The largest volunteer day on the planet.

Saturday, September 22, 2018 9:00am until 12:00pm  

Biggest and best local beach and diver cleanup. Free air fills, half price gear rental, and fabulous prizes for most unusual finds. Please pre-register to save time at the event - for Releases & Parking Pass (Download & Print) Arrive early for the best parking! Join us at the Malibu Pier.
  
JOIN our 2018 FACEBOOK EVENT for the latest news & photos of the event! Prizes for Best finds! 

Can't make the event?  Or want to donate? CLICK HERE!
 REMINDERS for Saturday's dive:
1) Register - go to link MALIBU PIER; save time by downloading the CCD waiver & Dive release, sign & bring with you. (Divers under 18 must have their waiver signed by parent or guardian.) REGISTER https://www.eventbrite.com/e/coastal-cleanup-day-2018-tickets-48460603971 CCD EVENT WAIVER https://healthebay.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/Waiver-English-2018.pdf DIVE EVENT RELEASE http://www.malibudivers.com/pdf/2018-CCD_10085_SpecEvent%20LiaRelFrm_501_FF.pdf PARKING PASS http://www.malibudivers.com/pdf/2018-Parking-Pass_20180922.pdf 2) Arrive on time - dive briefing will be right before 9 am entry time. Make sure to print out a parking pass - see above. Location: Pier/Tower Mal 1 | 23000 Pacific Coast Hwy, Malibu, 90265 (west or left below parking lot of Pier)

3) Pack your dive gear the night before - while our store opens at 8 am, it is best to rent the day before (50% off rental with proof of your pre-registration). Also, bring a reusable game bag or other reusable bag for trash collection; we do NOT have any bags to give out.
4) Bring: water, snacks, dive gear & tank, weights, towel, CCD waiver, Malibu Divers Dive release, parking pass, etc.
