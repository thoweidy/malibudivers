Memorial Day Camp & Dive Weekend

For the best MEMORIAL WEEKEND and vacation in Southern California!  

  
Introducing Los Angeles / Catalina Island vacation for this summer for Freedivers and SCUBA Divers that can include friends and families (divers and non-divers). Join us at Emerald Bay on the West End of Catalina Island immersed in the kelp forests and rock reefs that make California diving so distinct. This is a weekend for diving, learning new skills, and enjoying outdoor activities on the remote west end of Catalina Island. 

To Register - use the SIGN UP link now as SPACE IS NOW LIMITED & WILL SELLOUT!

 or click this link 
  

$375* All-Inclusive Diving & Camping Package: 
ALL INCLUSIVE PACKAGE includes:
  • Round trip ferry boat directly to Emerald Bay, 
  • 6 Meals, 
  • Shared Cabin, 
  • Tanks & Weights, up to 6 dives; 5 day time & 1 night dive (you must have proof of night dive training or dive with a Pro for night dive)  
  • Dive spots include Doctor's Cove, Emerald Bay, Indian Rock, Wreck Dive, and more.
Yes, you can add even more dives, upgrade to boat dives, and more night dives.
+ More Additional dives: $50
+ More Night dives: $70
   
Yes, you can add more training and experience with a Dive Pro:
  • Get Open Water Certified - do your class and pool before you come. 
  • + Add Adventure or Advanced Open Water dives
  • + Specialties - Drysuit, Deep, Night, Navigation, Search & Recovery, Boat, Drift, Fish id, Naturalist, Shark Awareness
  • + Add Freediving (proof of training and certification required)
Camping fun activities include:

• Kayaking - take a Kayak and dive from it!
• Canoeing
• Hiking
• Swimming, Stand-up paddle boarding
• BB Gun Shooting
• Aquarium Tours - at the Pennington Marine Center
• Campfires - it wouldn't be camping without one!
• Night Dive (certified divers)
• Assigned Cabin Housing by group or gender
• Ferry Transportation right to Emerald Bay
• All 6 Meals in Camp (Saturday - lunch & dinner, Sunday -breakfast, lunch & dinner, Monday - breakfast)
 
$275 All-Inclusive Non-Diver Camp Package:
ALL INCLUSIVE PACKAGE includes round trip ferry boat to Emerald Bay, Meals, Cabin, and Camp fun activities (above).  Non-divers can try scuba by adding Discover Scuba Diving partial day program (DSD) by pre-registering or at Camp.
  • Add Introductory SCUBA dive $125, additional dives $50

  • Coming over on your own Private yacht? Use the food and facilities option and/or if you want to also add the dive package. Call 310-510-4253 for mooring information. Moorings will go quickly, so it will be challenging for Memorial Weekend as this is the place to be to have a Catalina vacation. 
  
HOW TO REGISTER: in our ONLINE Store or Buy Tickets thru Malibu Divers! (310) 456-2396

SCUBA RENTAL GEAR is available for an additional charge at Emerald Bay - tanks and weights are included for this event.  Please keep in mind, there is a limited supply, so availability and fit are not guaranteed.

HOUSING: All participants will be in Cabins. Upgrades to individual cabins (3 beds per room, 1.5 bunks) are available and limited in number for $120 per room up to 3 people for entire weekend.

Participants can email info@malibudivers.com for more information about the accommodations.  Checkout the About Camp Emerald Bay page, the Camp Map and photos in our Gallery.
